Auditorium

Kingston Engineering College is home to a state-of-the-art auditorium that serves as the central hub for cultural, academic, and ceremonial events across the campus. The auditorium is designed with world-class acoustics, modern lighting systems, and an elegant interior that creates an impressive atmosphere for all occasions.

With a seating capacity of over 1,200 people, the main auditorium is equipped with advanced audio-visual systems, LED projection screens, and a fully furnished stage that can accommodate large-scale performances, seminars, conferences, and convocation ceremonies. The infrastructure meets international standards ensuring a premium experience for every event.

The facility is also equipped with modern climate control systems, high-speed internet connectivity, and professional stage management equipment, making it ideal for both formal academic events and vibrant cultural celebrations.
