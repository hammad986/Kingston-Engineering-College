About Us

Kingston Engineering College, located in the sprawling campus of 11.62 acres in Vellore City, Chittoor National Highway is just 5km away from Katpadi Railway Station. It was started under the flagship of institution M/S. Duraimurugan Educational Trust, approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.

ABOUT US

Why Choose Kingston

At Kingston Engineering College, we blend academic rigor with real-world experience. Our mission is to nurture innovation, leadership, and a spirit of inquiry among students through industry-relevant education and value-driven learning.

  • PhD faculty predominantly hold degrees from top-tier institutes such as IITs & NITs.
  • Rural & National immersion Programs
  • 7-day paid international study tour Programs (subsidised for golden scholar batch)
  • 25 industry study tours

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a pioneer in engineering and management education by providing amicable ambience and being a centre for learning thereby perpetuating versatile professionals in engineering and management suitable for industry, higher education and research.

Our Mission

  • Developing, nurturing and building professionals with technical, research and managerial expertise, intellectual proficiency, right attitude and moral ethics.
  • Building infrastructure that earmarks the institution to be the most preferred educational destination and provides the right ambience for creative and innovative teaching - learning process.
  • Enabling learners to broaden their horizon and helping to meet global challenges.
  • Empowering students to lead with ethics, drive sustainable development, and make transformative contributions to society and industry.

Our Core Values

  • EXCELLENCE : We create value through hard work, effective teaching, and academic excellence.
  • CREATIVITY : Fresh ideas, bold thinking, and an entrepreneurial spirit keep us creative.
  • EMPATHY & COMPASSION : Our core values and primary focus are on uplifting the human condition in all endeavours of life.
  • INCLUSION : We foster a welcoming culture that empowers diverse talent to excel.
  • INTEGRITY : We uphold the highest standards of ethics, transparency, and accountability.
