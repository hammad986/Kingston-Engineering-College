Main Building - Administrative Hub

The heart of Kingston Engineering College features state-of-the-art classrooms with interactive whiteboards, comfortable seating, and excellent acoustics. Smart classrooms are equipped with video conferencing facilities for collaborative learning with international institutions.

Classrooms 45+ Modern Classrooms
Capacity 45-60 students each
Technology Smart Board + WiFi

Computer Labs - Learning Hubs

Kingston houses 12 fully equipped computer labs with the latest software and hardware. Each lab is designed for specialized skills - from basic programming to advanced AI/ML. Labs are accessible 24/7 with dedicated lab assistants and mentors.

Number of Labs 12 Specialized Labs
Systems per Lab 50+ High-end PCs
Availability 24x7 Access

Central Library - Knowledge Center

Our 5-story library houses over 50,000 books, 200 journals, and access to multiple online databases. It features comfortable reading zones, individual study cubicles, group discussion rooms, and a digital library with e-books and research papers.

Books Collection 50,000+ Titles
Journals 200+ Subscriptions
E-Resources 10+ Databases

Sports Complex - Wellness Center

Spread across 10 acres, Kingston's sports complex includes cricket ground, basketball court, volleyball court, tennis court, badminton court, gym, and swimming pool. Professional coaches and fitness trainers are available for guidance.

Area 10 Acres
Facilities 8 Sports Types
Coaches Professional Staff

Hostels - Home Away From Home

Kingston provides comfortable hostel accommodation for boys and girls with separate facilities. Each room has high-speed WiFi, adequate furniture, security systems, and 24-hour surveillance. Common areas include recreation rooms, dining halls, and laundry facilities.

Total Capacity 2000+ Students
Room Types Single, Dual, Triple
Security 24x7 Monitored

Cafeteria & Mess Facilities

Multi-cuisine cafeteria serving Indian, Chinese, and continental food. High-quality ingredients, hygienic food preparation, and professional chefs ensure healthy and delicious meals for all students and staff members throughout the day.

Seating Capacity 800+ Persons
Cuisines Multi-Cuisine
Operating Hours 6 AM - 10 PM
