About Kingston Engineering College

Kingston Engineering College, established in 2001, is an autonomous institution approved by AICTE & Council of Architecture, New Delhi, affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The institution has been accredited with 'A' Grade by NAAC and recognized under Section 2(f) & 12(B) of UGC Act, 1956.

Located on Chittoor Main Road, Vellore, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across 11 departments with a strong focus on industry-ready education, research, and innovation. All eligible programs are accredited by NBA, and the institution is certified with ISO 21001:2018.
